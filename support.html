Bug Reports

If you've encountered a bug or issue with the app, please include the following information in your report:

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version (found in Settings)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)
  • Any error messages you've seen

Feature Requests

We love hearing your ideas! If you have a feature request, please describe:

  • The feature you'd like to see
  • How it would improve your experience
  • Any specific use cases or scenarios

While we can't implement every request, we carefully consider all feedback and prioritize features that will benefit the most users.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent issues, please mark your email as urgent or use the subject line "URGENT" to help us prioritize your request.

App Store Reviews

If you're enjoying Replex, we'd greatly appreciate a review on the App Store! Your feedback helps us improve and helps other users discover the app. If you've had a negative experience, please contact us directly first so we can help resolve any issues.
