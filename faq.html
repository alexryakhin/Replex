Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Replex. If you can't find what you're looking for, please contact our support team.

What is Replex?
Replex is your all-in-one workout tracking and planning app designed to help you achieve your fitness goals. Start instantly with ready-made workout templates or create your own custom routines. Track every exercise with 150+ exercises in the library, see which muscles you're working with our interactive muscle map, and monitor your progress over time. All your data syncs automatically across all your iPhones using iCloud, so you can train anywhere.
Is Replex free to use?
Replex offers a 7-day free trial so you can experience all features before committing. After the trial period, a subscription is required to continue using the app. We offer both Monthly and Annual subscription options. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store, and you can cancel at any time.
What subscription options are available?
Replex offers two subscription options: Monthly and Annual. Both subscriptions unlock all features of the app. The Annual subscription provides better value with a discount compared to the monthly plan. All subscriptions include a 7-day free trial period. You can manage your subscription through your App Store account settings.
How does the free trial work?
When you first download Replex, you'll receive a 7-day free trial that gives you full access to all features. A payment method (credit card) is required to start the trial, as required by Apple's App Store policies. At the end of the 7-day period, you'll need to subscribe to continue using the app. You can cancel anytime during the trial period and you won't be charged - the trial will end and your subscription won't start. If you don't cancel, the trial automatically converts to a paid subscription at the end of the trial period.
What happens when my subscription expires?
Replex uses a subscription model where access to the app requires an active subscription. If your subscription expires or is cancelled, you will no longer be able to access the app's features. Your workout data remains stored in iCloud and will be accessible again if you resubscribe. We recommend managing your subscription through the App Store to ensure uninterrupted access.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, so you'll continue to have access until then. If you cancel during the free trial period, you won't be charged. Refunds are handled according to Apple's App Store refund policy. For subscription management, go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone.
Does Replex work with Apple Watch?
Yes! Replex has a full-featured Apple Watch companion app that allows you to track workouts directly from your wrist. You can start, pause, and complete entire workouts without needing your iPhone nearby. The Watch app displays real-time metrics including heart rate, calories burned, and elapsed time. It features an automatic rest timer with haptic alerts, smart rest modes (timer-based or heart rate recovery), and seamless HealthKit integration. You can also mirror workouts to your iPhone for a larger display view. Watch face complications provide quick access to start workouts right from your wrist.
What analytics features are available?
Replex includes a comprehensive Analytics Dashboard that helps you track and understand your fitness progress. Features include: workout frequency trends with interactive activity charts, strength gains tracking with exercise-specific progress monitoring, muscle group distribution analysis to identify training imbalances, workout streaks and completion rates over time, performance comparison across different time periods (week, month, 3 months, all time), and volume trend analysis to see your training load over time. All analytics are visualized with easy-to-understand charts and graphs.
How does iCloud sync work?
Replex uses Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services to synchronize your workout data across all your iPhones signed in to the same Apple ID. When you enable iCloud sync (which is typically automatic), your workouts, exercises, templates, and progress are securely stored in your iCloud account and automatically synced to all your iPhones. You can manage iCloud sync through your device's Settings app under iCloud.
Can I use Replex without iCloud?
Yes, you can disable iCloud sync through your device's Settings app. When iCloud is disabled, your data will be stored locally on your device only. However, you won't be able to sync data across multiple iPhones.
How many exercises are included in the app?
Replex includes over 150 exercises across all major muscle groups. Exercises are organized by muscle groups, equipment type, and exercise category, making it easy to find what you're looking for.
Can I create my own workout templates?
Absolutely! While Replex includes many ready-made workout templates to get you started, you can also create completely custom workout templates that fit your specific goals and preferences. Save your favorite routines for quick access, duplicate and modify existing templates, organize them however you want, and track personal records for every exercise.
What is the muscle map visualization?
The muscle map visualization is an interactive feature that highlights which muscle groups are targeted by each exercise. It shows both primary and secondary muscle groups, helping you understand how exercises work your body and plan balanced workouts.
Can I schedule workouts in my calendar?
Yes! Replex integrates with Apple's Calendar app using EventKit. You can schedule workouts and they will appear in your system calendar. You can manage calendar permissions through your device's Settings app.
Does Replex track my location?
Location tracking is completely optional. If you choose to enable it, Replex can save the GPS location where you performed a workout. This feature requires your explicit permission and can be disabled at any time through your device's Settings app.
What languages does Replex support?
Replex supports 50+ languages! This includes English, Spanish, French, German, Russian, Ukrainian, Polish, Czech, Turkish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Greek, Romanian, Hungarian, Hebrew, Hindi, Thai, Vietnamese, and many more. The app automatically uses your device's language settings, and everything is fully translated - the interface, exercise names, workout templates, and all features.
Does Replex include ready-made workout templates?
Yes! Replex includes a full library of expertly designed workout templates. Choose from popular splits like Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, Full Body, and many more. Each template comes in different versions based on your equipment - whether you have access to a full gym, work out at home, use resistance bands, or prefer bodyweight calisthenics. You can browse and add these templates directly from the Today screen to get started instantly.
What workout splits are available?
Replex includes templates for all the most popular workout splits including Push/Pull/Legs (PPL), Upper/Lower, Full Body, Push Day (chest, shoulders, triceps), Pull Day (back and biceps), Leg Day, Chest & Triceps, Back & Biceps, Shoulders & Arms, Core Focus, and Calisthenics Full Body. Each split is available for multiple equipment types so you can train anywhere.
Can I filter exercises by equipment type?
Absolutely! Replex lets you filter exercises by the equipment you have available. Whether you're at a full gym with all equipment, at home with minimal equipment, using resistance bands, or doing bodyweight exercises, you can quickly find exercises that work with what you have. This makes it easy to plan effective workouts regardless of your equipment situation.
Does Replex support dark mode?
Yes! Replex fully supports both light and dark modes and automatically adapts to your device's system theme settings.
How do I export my workout data?
You can export your data through the app's Settings. The export functionality allows you to create a backup of your workouts and exercise data. For specific instructions, please check the Settings section within the app.
What devices are supported?
Replex is designed for iPhone. It requires iOS 17.0 or later.
Is my data secure?
Yes, your data security is a top priority. When using iCloud sync, your data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud services. Local data is stored securely on your device using Core Data. We do not share your personal workout data with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (such as through Apple's iCloud services).
Can I use Replex offline?
Yes! Replex works offline. All core features are available without an internet connection. However, iCloud sync requires an internet connection to synchronize data across your iPhones.
How do I track my progress?
Replex automatically tracks your workout completion, duration, and personal records. You can view your progress through the app's statistics and progress tracking features. The app also tracks completion status for each workout and exercise.
What if I lose my data?
If you have iCloud sync enabled, your data is automatically backed up to your iCloud account. If you need to restore data, it should automatically sync when you sign in with the same Apple ID. If you're experiencing data loss issues, please contact our support team immediately.
Can I filter exercises by equipment?
Yes! Replex includes an equipment filter that allows you to find exercises based on the equipment you have available. This makes it easy to plan workouts whether you're at a fully equipped gym or working out at home with limited equipment.
How do I report a bug or issue?
If you encounter a bug or issue, please contact our support team through the Support page. Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots or screen recordings are also helpful.
Can I suggest new features?
Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Please send your feature requests through our Support page. While we can't implement every suggestion, we carefully consider all feedback and prioritize features that will benefit the most users.
Is Replex a medical or health service?
No, Replex is not a medical or health service provider. The app is intended for informational and tracking purposes only. It is not intended to be a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or fitness program.
How do I delete my account or data?
You can delete the app from your device, which will remove all local data. If you have iCloud sync enabled, you may also need to delete the data from your iCloud account through your device's Settings. For assistance with complete data deletion, please contact our support team.
