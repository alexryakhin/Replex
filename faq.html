Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about plans, features, sync, and support.

Is Replex free?
Yes. Core workout tracking is free.
What does premium include?
Apple Watch workouts, advanced analytics, unlimited/custom templates, and calendar scheduling.
Is there a premium trial?
Yes. Premium includes a 7-day free trial.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Manage billing and cancellation in App Store settings.
What happens when premium ends?
You keep your data and continue using free features.
Does Replex support Apple Watch?
Yes, with premium.
How does iCloud sync work?
If enabled, data syncs across Apple devices using the same Apple ID.
Can I use Replex offline?
Yes. Core workout flows work offline.
How many exercises are included?
Replex includes 150+ exercises.
Does Replex support dark mode?
Yes. It supports light and dark mode.
How do I report bugs or request features?
Use the support page and include device, iOS, app version, and details.
